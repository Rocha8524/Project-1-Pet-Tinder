About Us

Hello and welcome to Pet Tinder. We are a group of dedicated and passionate animal enthusiasts who wants to make it easier for anyone looking for a pet to care for. We have partnered up with local breeders and animal shelters to ensure that the animals listed on the website are happy and healthy. We take time to ensure that any shelter and breeder we do buisness with are following state laws and are treating the animals in their temporary care with high ethical standards. If you are looking for a pet to love and are willing to provide them a loving environment, then choose the animal you wish to have and you will be given a selection of the breeds we have available.
